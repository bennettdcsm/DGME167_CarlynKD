PROJECT TYPE

Volunteer Connect is a mobile-first website that helps people find volunteering opportunities that match their skills, interests, and personal schedule.

ROLE

I was the Project Manager on a team of 3. The roles of my teammates were Design Manager and Research Lead. I kept the project on track, managed deadlines and deliverables. I designed the logo and collaborated on the branding direction.

OBJECTIVE

Create a product to increase community engagement within the community.

APPROACH

Research - surveys, interviews, and competitor analysis
Data Synthesis and Analysis - personas and journey map
Ideation - brainstorm, sketch and design
Test - usability studies
Iterate - test and redesign

RESULT
