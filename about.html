About
My name is Carlyn Kuchan and I founded Carlyn’s Creations in 2024. Our mission is to create beautiful digital designs that are effortless to navigate.

Education
I have a Bachelor of Science degree in Interior Design from the University of Minnesota. After working in the Interior Design industry for a decade, I decided to learn about Web, Graphic, and UI/UX Design at the College of San Mateo. I am currently working on my Web and Mobile Design certificate.

Hobbies
On my free time, I enjoy getting out in nature with my dog and my husband. I will try almost any kind of outdoor adventure! I regularly go hiking, mountain biking, and gravel biking. In the winter, my favorite activity is downhill skiing. When I’m not spending my free time enjoying nature, I like to listen to music, go to concerts, watch movies, and work on craft projects at home.
